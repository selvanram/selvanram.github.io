Privacy Policy for Timerpal

Effective date: March 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Timerpal ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our interval timer application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Timerpal does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or postal address from within the app.
  • Usage Data: We may collect non-personal information about how the app is used (for example, device type, operating system version, and basic performance metrics) to diagnose issues and improve the app.

3. How We Use Your Information

Any non-personal usage data is used solely to maintain and improve Timerpal’s performance and user experience. We do not use collected data to profile users or for targeted advertising.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Timerpal is a standalone interval timer and does not use cookies, web beacons, or other tracking technologies.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not share your information with third parties for their own uses. Timerpal does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, or other third-party trackers by default.

6. Data Retention

Since Timerpal does not collect personal information, there is no personal data retention. Usage data (if any) is retained only as needed to improve the app and diagnose issues.

7. Children’s Privacy

Timerpal is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any data we process, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you believe your device or data is compromised, please stop using the app and contact us.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we will update the "Effective date" at the top of this page. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at privacy@timerpal.example.
